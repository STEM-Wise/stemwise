Our Team

Founder & Course Director


Name: Gabria Gordon
School: Immaculate Conception High School
About: Gabria, a passionate STEM enthusiast and a grade 12 student at Immaculate Conception High School, specializes in Website Development, Python, and C Programming. In addition to her technical skills, she is also the Founder of the STEM-Wise initiative. By establishing this initiative, she aims to bridge the gap in STEM education and foster a passion for the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics among young learners. Gabria's ultimate goal is to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in an increasingly technologically advanced world.

She has been appointed as the course director for the upcoming 'Fundamentals of Python' course, scheduled for this summer.
